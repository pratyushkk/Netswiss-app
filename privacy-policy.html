Privacy Policy

NetSwiss — com.netswiss.app

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NetSwiss ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

NetSwiss does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app functions entirely on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

NetSwiss does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data (GPS coordinates used for Mock GPS stay only on your device)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Network usage data or browsing history
  • Any analytics or crash data sent to external servers

2. Permissions Used

The App requests certain Android permissions solely to provide its core features:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / MOCK_LOCATION: Required to enable the Mock GPS feature. No location data is transmitted off-device.
  • CHANGE_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to read network type and signal information for the Network Dashboard and Speed Monitor.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to run the Live Speed Monitor notification in the status bar.
  • WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS: Required to access hidden system settings for the Force 5G Mode feature (granted via ADB by the user).

All permissions are used strictly on-device and no data derived from these permissions is sent to any server.

3. Data Storage

NetSwiss does not use any external databases, cloud storage, or third-party analytics services. Any preferences or settings you configure in the app are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

NetSwiss does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics libraries, or social media plugins. There are no external network calls made by the app.

5. Children's Privacy

NetSwiss does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not target children and contains no content directed at minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
